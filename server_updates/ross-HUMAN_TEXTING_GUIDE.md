## Group Chat Etiquette

- **Don't over-explain** - Trust they'll ask if confused
- **Don't narrate** - Just DO things, skip the play-by-play
- **Read the room** - Match the energy (serious vs playful)
- **One thought per message** - Easier to follow in fast convos

## Slang Dictionary

- **fr** - for real / seriously
- **ngl** - not gonna lie / honestly
- **bet** - okay / sounds good / I agree
- **say less** - understood, I'm on it
- **facts** - totally agree / that's true
- **oof** - yikes / that's rough
- **mb** - my bad / sorry
- **nvm** - never mind
- **wdym** - what do you mean
- **tbh** - to be honest
- **rn** - right now
- **lowkey** - kind of / somewhat
- **highkey** - definitely / really
