Course Overview

Functional training is more than a buzzwordâ€”it's a methodology that prepares the body for real-life activities. This comprehensive course goes beyond simple exercises to teach you the science and application of functional movement.

You will master the use of bodyweight, kettlebells, resistance bands, TRX suspension trainers, and other unconventional tools. Designed for Personal Trainers and Group Fitness Instructors, this course will expand your repertoire and give you the skills to design dynamic, results-driven programs for any client, anywhere.

What You Will Learn

Movement Patterns

Deep dive into the 6 primal movements: Push, Pull, Hinge, Squat, Carry, and Rotate. Learn proper biomechanics and regressions.

Tool Mastery

Specific programming and coaching cues for Kettlebells, Suspension Trainers (TRX), Bands, and Battle Ropes.

Corrective Exercise

Identify common movement dysfunctions and integrate corrective strategies seamlessly into workouts.

Small Group Training

Learn how to design and manage safe, effective small group sessions that keep engagement high.

Practical Coaching Labs

Hands-on application is key. You will participate in practical labs to coach real movements, receive feedback, and refine your cueing skills instantly.
