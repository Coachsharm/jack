Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our courses, certifications, payment options, and what to expect when you join Fit Body Thrive.

General

Fit Body Thrive is Singapore's premier fitness education academy founded by Coach Sharm. We specialise in evidence-based courses for Personal Trainers and fitness enthusiasts, focusing on three core pillars: Fitness, Nutrition, and Psychology. Our mission is to elevate the standard of coaching in the industry.
Our courses are designed for a wide range of individuals: experienced Personal Trainers looking to upskill and earn CECs, aspiring coaches starting their careers, and fitness enthusiasts who simply want to deepen their own knowledge and training mastery.
CECs (Continuing Education Credits) are recognised units used to maintain your professional certifications with major international bodies like ACE, NASM, and others. Completing our courses provides you with these necessary credits to keep your qualifications current and valid.

Courses

Duration varies by the level of the course. Foundation courses typically run for 12 hours (approx. 1.5 weekends). Advanced courses are generally 16-20 hours. Our signature Specialist Certifications involve 24 hours of coursework plus a dedicated 4-week mentorship period.
We believe strongly in practical, hands-on learning. The majority of our courses are conducted in-person at our facility to ensure you master the physical skills. Some theoretical components or mentorship sessions may be delivered via hybrid or online formats for convenience.
Upon successful completion, you will receive a Body Thrive Certification (e.g., "Certified Transformation Specialist") which is a mark of quality in the industry. Additionally, you will receive a certificate of completion detailing the CECs earned for your recertification needs.
