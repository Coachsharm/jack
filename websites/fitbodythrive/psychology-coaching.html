From Instructor to Trusted Confidant

Most trainers are excellent at prescribing exercises but struggle when clients "get in their own way." When a client self-sabotages, ghosts you, or projects their stress onto the session, how do you handle it?

This course bridges the gap between physical training and psychological support. You will learn where the boundary lies between coaching and therapy, and how to use psychological frameworks to build rock-solid trust.

"The best coaches don't just transform bodies; they create a safe space for clients to transform their lives."
