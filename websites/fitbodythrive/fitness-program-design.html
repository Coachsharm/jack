Who Is This Course For?

This advanced module is designed for certified Personal Trainers who have mastered the basics but feel limited by generic "3 sets of 10" programming. If you struggle to keep long-term clients progressing or feel unsure how to periodise for specific goals like athletic performance or pure strength, this course bridges that gap.

  • Coaches wanting to move beyond "cookie-cutter" templates
  • Trainers working with clients hitting varying plateaus
  • Professionals preparing athletes or special population clients
  • PTs seeking ACE/NASM CECs for recertification

Course Curriculum

A deep dive into the art and science of exercise programming.

Periodisation Models

Understanding and applying Linear, Undulating, and Block periodisation for different client timelines.

  • Macro, Meso, and Microcycles
  • Linear vs Non-Linear Progression
  • Deloading strategies

Needs Analysis & Screening

Building programs based on assessment data, not guesswork. Identifying weak links and movement patterns.

  • Static & Dynamic Postural Assessment
  • Movement efficiency screening
  • Goal-specific needs analysis

Goal-Specific Programming

Designing specific protocols for the most common client requests.

  • Fat Loss (Metabolic Resistance Training)
  • Hypertrophy (Sarcoplasmic vs Myofibrillar)
  • Pure Strength & Power Development
  • Athletic Performance

Special Populations

Adapting standard programs for clients with unique physiological needs.

  • Active Ageing & Senior Fitness
  • Pre & Post-Natal Considerations
  • Youth Resistance Training

Case Studies & Review

Practical application of theory using real-world scenarios.

  • Critiquing real client programs
  • Troubleshooting plateaus
  • Long-term planning workshops
