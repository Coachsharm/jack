Stop Guessing. Start Guiding.

Many Personal Trainers feel uncertain when clients ask detailed nutrition questions. You want to help, but you're worried about giving the wrong advice or stepping outside your scope of practice.

This course bridges the gap. We strip away the fad diets and pseudo-science to give you a robust, evidence-based foundation in nutrition. You'll learn exactly how to calculate needs, structure meal plans, and guide clients towards sustainable results within the Singaporean context.

Course Curriculum

A comprehensive 12-hour dive into practical nutrition science.

Science Fundamentals

Deep dive into macronutrients, micronutrients, energy balance, and metabolism. Understand metabolic adaptation and why "calories in, calories out" is just the starting point.

Practical Meal Planning

Learn to build plans without reliance on meal replacements or extreme diets. Create sustainable strategies that clients can actually stick to long-term.

The Singapore Context

Real-world application: Navigating hawker centres, reading local food labels, and managing social eating in Singapore's food-centric culture.

Scope & Frameworks

Define clear boundaries for your practice. Learn to build client-friendly meal frameworks that educate and empower rather than just prescribe.
