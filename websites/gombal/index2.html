What is Gombal?

The Art of Sweet Talk

Gombal is Indonesia's beloved tradition of over-the-top, cheesy romantic lines. Think pick-up lines, but turned up to eleven with poetic Indonesian flair.

Cultural Phenomenon

From WhatsApp forwards to TikTok trends, gombal lines are shared millions of times daily. The cheesier the better — it's an endearing art form.

Intentionally Corny

The whole point is to be so ridiculously romantic it circles back to being charming. The worse the line, the bigger the smile. That's the magic.
