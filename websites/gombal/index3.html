What is Gombal?

Indonesia's beloved art of cheesy romance

01

Sweet Talk Tradition

Gombal is the Indonesian tradition of over-the-top, poetically cheesy romantic lines. Think pick-up lines infused with Bahasa Indonesia's natural beauty and warmth.

02

Cultural Phenomenon

From WhatsApp forwards to viral TikToks, gombal lines are shared millions of times daily across Southeast Asia. The cheesier, the better.

03

The Charm Factor

The magic of gombal is being so ridiculously romantic it circles back to being charming. The worse the line, the bigger the smile. That's the whole art form.
