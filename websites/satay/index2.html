Our Heritage

NOT JUST FOOD.
A RITUAL.

In a world of gas grills and shortcuts, we choose the hard way. The charcoal way.

Our founder, Uncle Lim, started with a single pushcart in 1982. His philosophy was simple: fire is a flavor. You can't fake the smoke. You can't rush the caramelization.

Every morning at 4 AM, we pound fresh lemongrass, turmeric, and galangal. By 5 PM, the coals are glowing red. This isn't fast food. This is slow, deliberate, delicious art.

The Craft

FROM SPICE TO SMOKE

01

The Marinade

Fresh turmeric, lemongrass, coriander, and cumin. Ground daily. Marinated for 12 hours minimum to penetrate the meat fibers.

02

The Skewering

Hand-threaded by our aunties with decades of experience. Just the right amount of fat-to-meat ratio for juiciness.

03

The Fire

Grilled over mangrove wood charcoal. Fanned constantly to control the heat. The result? That signature smoky char.
