About Mdm Nisha

Dedicated leadership in holistic student development since 1998.

As the Head of Department (HOD) for Physical Education, Co-Curricular Activities, and Aesthetics, I have spent over 25 years fostering an environment where students can thrive physically, creatively, and socially.

My role extends beyond administration; I am deeply involved in the day-to-day growth of our students. Serving as a form teacher for Primary 3 and Primary 4 cohorts allows me to stay grounded in the student experience, ensuring that our departmental programmes translate into meaningful outcomes for every child.

Working alongside HODs for English, Mother Tongue, Mathematics, Science, and CCE, I contribute to a unified leadership team committed to the high standards of the Singapore Education Service.
