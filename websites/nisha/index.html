About Mdm Nisha

As the Head of Department (HOD) for Physical Education, Co-Curricular Activities (CCA), and Aesthetics at Gong Shang Primary School, I have dedicated my career to fostering a balanced and enriching educational environment.

Serving in this leadership position since 1998, I bring over 25 years of specialized experience in overseeing the school's diverse CCA portfolio, PE programmes, and aesthetic subjects including music and visual arts.

Beyond departmental leadership, I remain deeply connected to student development as a form teacher for Primary 3 and Primary 4 cohorts, ensuring that every child receives the guidance they need to thrive within the Ministry of Education (MOE) system.
