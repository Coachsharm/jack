About Me

Orchestrating balance in student development.

Since 1998, I have served as the Head of Department overseeing the intersection of physical vitality, artistic appreciation, and co-curricular engagement.

Beyond the strategic oversight of departmental programmes, I remain deeply rooted in the classroom as a Form Teacher for Primary 3 and 4 students. This dual perspective ensures that our broad policies always translate into meaningful, personal growth for every child.

As part of the MOE professional teaching service, I collaborate intimately with HODs across English, Math, Mother Tongue, and Science to deliver a truly unified curriculum.
